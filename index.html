2026 Technical Reporting

Resources, guidance and tools to support results reporting across CGIAR.

Technical reporting is a core CGIAR accountability requirement, enabling the System Organization to meet its obligations to funders, governance bodies, and stakeholders. These obligations are established through the Charter of the CGIAR System Organization, and are operationalized through the Performance and Results Management Framework and the Technical Reporting Arrangement 2025–2030.

As a key step of CGIAR's Adaptive Management Cycle, technical reporting brings together results from Programs & Accelerators and Centers — including W1/W2 and mapped W3/bilateral projects — to assess performance, support learning, and inform decision-making.

It culminates in CGIAR's reporting products: the Program & Accelerator Annual Technical Reports, the Results Dashboard, and the Portfolio Narrative.
